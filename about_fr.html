About Alison Learn more about the project

Alison originates from an INSA student project. It was started in late 2018 with a 6-month research project, and was continued in 2019 to develop a full solution.

Watch project video

Project History

The Alison Project originates from a 4th Year research project at INSA Toulouse, pursued from October 2018 to June 2019. It's an initiative of the GEI Department, and the outline was given to us by our tutors. You can find more information about the original project by visiting the Legacy website.

It got picked up again in October 2019, when three students of the original team proposed to INSA to continue the project for 5th year. This resulted in the constitution of a new project team, more focused on building a finished product and making it available to the world.
