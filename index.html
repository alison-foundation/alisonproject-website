A state-of-the-art sound recognition solution designed to help people suffering from hearing impairement or deafness all over the world.

Record the sounds you want using our cross-platform mobile app. Alison will do the rest.

Clean design

Alison comes with a simple, beautiful design that you can put anywhere in your home. The object lights up when it catches a sound, and alerts you in a visual way.

Android App

To set up Alison and record the sounds you want to recognize, you need to download our simple calibration application.

The app is available on Android, and only requires you to connect to Alison via Bluetooth. The calibration process is really quick and simple, and you will be ready to go in less than 5 minutes!

An iOS version of the app is not in development at the moment but could be considered in the future.
